# Climate Campaign: The Ice Cubes Have Melted

**Tagline**: "The Ice Cubes have melted. The Sea is Starting to Bubble! We Have To Turn The Thermostat Down!"

## Overview

A climate advocacy campaign targeting economic power for systemic change.

## Mission

If governments can ration oil, food, and materials for war, they can ration them to prevent war and climate catastrophe. This campaign appeals to those with economic power to take action.

## Target Audiences

- **The Ultra-Wealthy**: Divest, invest in green transition, use political access
- **The Affluent**: Shift investment strategy, public advocacy
- **The Upper-Middle Class**: Conscious consumption, pressure fund managers
- **The Economically Influential**: Use platform for systemic change
- **The Working Class**: Sell car (if possible), reduce oil spending, organize rideshares
